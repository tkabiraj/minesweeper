How to Play 

Goal

To uncover all the cells without stepping on a mine .

Rules

You can only click on these cells . These cells are empty cells, which means they have no bombs surrounding them.

If you click on a cell and a number appears, that means there are that many bombs surrounding that cell.

As you can see in the example, once you figure out where the mine is, you can right click on that cell and flag it .

If you are unsure about a cell, you can right click it twice and come back to it later.

Once all the cells are correctly flagged, numbered, or blank, a smiley face with sunglasses will appear .

Otherwise, you would have stepped on a mine and this will appear on that cell .
